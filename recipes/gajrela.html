Gajrela

Carrot halwa is a popular sweet made in North India, and it is especially popular during winters. The traditional gajar ka halwa recipe is made with only carrots, whole (full-fat) milk, ghee and sugar; thereâ€™s no need for khoya (evaporated milk solids) or condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Cardmom Powder
  • Almonds
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. Boil the milk well.
  2. Once milk is boiled, add green cardamom and grated carrots and cook at medium flame.
  3. Once carrots become soft, press soaked rice with fingers, then add to milk and stir with spoon.
  4. Check if rice are tender, then add sugar and mix well.
  5. Cook at low flame.
  6. Once gajrela becomes thick, add chopped almonds and mix with spoon.
  7. Break rice with spoon so that it merges well in gajrela.
  8. Once it comes to desired consistency, remove from stove.
  9. Dish out and garnish with almonds and pistachios.
  10. Gajrela is ready.
  11. You can enjoy it either hot or cold, as you like!
